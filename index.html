My DISC Assesment

The DISC Assesment measures one's preferred Behavioral style and Values Index. D is for Decisive, I is for Interactive, S is for Stabilization, and C is for Cautious. Typically coders tend to be higher S and C. However, as you can see that I am a very high D and I! "But Cody, aren't high S and C's good for coding as they will be more heads down, predictable, and systematic?" Absolutely! S and C are very common among coders. "So what is special about being a D I coder?" Because the D I Coder is the rare breed! The diamond in the rough! One who has a logical brain to build, work through, and understand code, but also can interact with the team, leadership, and of course THE CLIENT! The D I coder is one you want in the meetings to communicate with leadership and pitch clients with technical explainations when necessary. D I's are also the future managers and the best trainers! When you find a D I coder- grab ahold of them- and don't let go!

So take my expereince with business, sales, and management and put that with my technical skills of Front End design and Back End framework- you will have an amazing team player, one hungry to always learn, future training and leader!
